bl_growthairfoil_bl_growthSubroutine
calc_bl_growthairfoil_bl_growthSubroutine

Calculates boundary layer growth on an airfoil (modeled as an ellipse), beginning at a stagnation point. Uses Thwaite's method for the laminar flow region, Michel's method to correct transition, and Head's method for the turbulent flow region.

veairfoil_bl_growthFunction

VE of the ellipse, $$ v_e = \left(1 + \tau\right) \cdot \sqrt{\frac{1 - x_i^2}{1-\left(1-\tau^2\right)x_i^2}} $$

xairfoil_bl_growthFunction

X-coordinates of the ellipse, $$ x_i = -\cos\left(\frac{(i-1)\pi}{nx-1}\tau\right) $$

yairfoil_bl_growthFunction

Y-coordinates of the ellipse, $$ y_i = \sin\left(\frac{(i-1)\pi}{nx-1}\tau\right) $$
